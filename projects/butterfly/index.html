Butterfly

Email for the new age


What is Butterfly?

Butterfly is a new email protocol that has been designed from scratch to take modern standards into
account, to be simple in design and also modular in terms of mail filtering by using bester as a backend
for message filtering (both inbound and outbound mail).

Why use it?

The old email protocols namely SMTP and IMAP/POP, firstly, are two separate protocols for what
should be a single protocol - a protocol for email.

Secondly, the single protocol that butterfly is, is very simple too and easy to manipulate too due
to it being based on JSON.
